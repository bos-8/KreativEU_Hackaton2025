Hackathon 2025 Acknowledgement
----------------------------------------------------------------------

This project "WandrEU" was created during **Hackathon 2025** (18–19.09.2025)
as a collaborative prototype to explore innovative solutions in web, AI, and cultural travel planning.

The project demonstrates integration of:
- Next.js frontend with multi-lingual support
- FastAPI backend with AI-powered itinerary planning (genetic algorithm)
- Cloud-native design principles for scalability

## Team: 3U-nity Vision

**3U** stands for *Free You*, symbolizing freedom and individuality.
It also reflects *EU* (European Union), representing unity and cooperation.
**Vision** points toward a shared, sustainable future for Europe.

## Acknowledgements
Special thanks to **Hackathon 2025 organizers**, mentors, and the **CreativEU program**
for inspiration, resources, and support during the event.

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 15 with Tailwind CSS & next-intl (multi-language frontend)
- FastAPI backend with genetic algorithm (AI-powered itinerary planning)
- Cloud-native design & deployment principles

## Disclaimer
This is a prototype created during a 24h hackathon.
It is **not production-ready** and may contain experimental or incomplete code.
Future work may include optimizations, security hardening, and production deployment.
